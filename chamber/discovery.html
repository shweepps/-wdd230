Discover Bloemfontein

About Bloemfontein

Bloemfontein is the capital city of the Free State province in South Africa. It is known as the "City of Roses" due to the abundance of rose gardens throughout the city. Bloemfontein is rich in history and culture, offering visitors a unique experience.

The city is home to several notable landmarks, including the historic City Hall, Naval Hill with its statue of Nelson Mandela, and the National Museum. Bloemfontein also hosts various events and festivals throughout the year, showcasing its vibrant arts and music scene.

The Free State is known for its wide open spaces, scenic landscapes, and warm hospitality. Bloemfontein serves as a gateway to explore the surrounding areas, including the stunning Golden Gate Highlands National Park and the charming towns of Clarens and Parys.
