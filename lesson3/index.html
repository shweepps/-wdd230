Website Planning Document

The site plan document describes the site name, site purpose, target audience, color scheme and application, typography and usually contains wire-frame sketch of the different views. In this Website, I included the following sections as pages:

Site Name

South African Chamber of Commerce and Industry

Site Purpose

The site purpose represent the business interests by influencing policy and legislative development and regulation. We engage government and legislatures or regulators in respect of certain of the regulations or pieces of legislation that we think would not be conducive for our business members or the economy in general or job creation. We are consulted in a lot of the policy that government looks at.

Target Audience

As the “Voice of Business” SACCI assists its members by preparing and submitting policy positions on business related issues to government. i.e. International Trade, Taxation, Economic Affairs, Labour and Company Wellness, Education & Training, Information Technology, Small Business, Transport and Regulatory Affairs. SACCI also interacts with many other agencies on business issues. During the year, SACCI holds various informative events and presentations using top Government and noteworthy business speakers.
