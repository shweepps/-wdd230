Carlsbad's History

In the early 1900s, Carlsbad was a well-known vacation destination for Hollywood celebrities seeking a retreat from the bustling city life. Famous actors, actresses, and filmmakers would flock to this charming coastal town to unwind and enjoy its natural beauty. One of the most legendary stories from that era involves a secret underground speakeasy that operated during the Prohibition era.

Bountiful foods's History

In the heart of the sun-kissed town of Carlsbad, nestled amidst lush orchards and verdant vineyards, the story of Bountiful Drinks Company began to unfold. Inspired by a shared love for refreshing and healthful beverages, two adventurous siblings, Alex and Emma Patterson, envisioned a place where the natural goodness of fresh fruits could be transformed into delightful, thirst-quenching drinks.


It all started in the early 1990s when the Patterson siblings inherited their family's longstanding fruit farm, Bountiful Orchards. Determined to honor their family's farming heritage while embracing innovation, Alex and Emma embarked on a journey to create something extraordinary. With boundless enthusiasm, they began experimenting with various fruit combinations, seeking the perfect balance of flavors that would tantalize taste buds and invigorate the senses. Their farmhouse kitchen became a laboratory of sorts, bubbling with mixtures of succulent berries, tangy citrus, and juicy tropical fruits.


After months of trial and error, they finally struck gold with their first signature creation: "Sunrise Splash." A vibrant blend of freshly squeezed oranges, sweet pineapple, and a hint of zesty lime, this invigorating drink became an instant hit among family and friends. Word about the Pattersons' divine elixirs spread like wildfire throughout Carlsbad. Soon, locals and tourists alike flocked to the humble fruit farm, eager to savor the natural goodness in every sip. The farm's quaint, open-air drink stand, adorned with twinkling fairy lights and shaded by fruit-laden trees, became a haven for those seeking respite from the sun.
